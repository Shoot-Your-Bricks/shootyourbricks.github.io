Why the Name?

Shoot Your Bricks is the name of my LEGO YouTube channel (shooting bricks with a camera). The YouTube channel was my first major presence on the internet, and I still use the name to this day in other internet contexts.


My real name is Luke, but sometimes I'm called by my nickname, Gunford, which is another reference to my YouTube channel (Gunford was a character that drove a cool LEGO car and caught bad guys).

My Interests

My biggest hobby, and most likely future career is software development.

I also have some video and audio editing experience with Davinci Resolve.

As mentioned, YouTubing is one of my hobbies. It's becoming obsolete as I don't see that it is worth the time invested, however I learned many things from it including video editing.

I still love LEGOs, and even though I don't spend much time on my YouTube channel, I still sell MOCs on my Rebrickable page.

I'm passionate about technology, especially products and start up companies that care about and focus on the user experience.
